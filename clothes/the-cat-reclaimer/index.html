The pages came from my sketchbook in which I developed the designs for The Cat Relaimer. The garments and accessories were designed for cat ladies. In a rush to care for cats there is no time to think about what to wear, clothes are just thrown on in a mismatched and ill-fitting, yet practical way. For someone who reclaims stray cats I have made everything from reclaimed materials.

Made by brandy nicole easter.
